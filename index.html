how to prevent

how to prevent virus

wash your place

wash your place everyday where to spend max. time in office and home & keep sanitize

maintain distance

Keep physical distance of at least 1 metre from others, even if they don’t appear to be sick. Avoid crowds and close contact.

don't touch face

Why? These mucous membranes are a portal of entry for germs that cause respiratory infections, including COVID-19.

wash your hand

During the Coronavirus Disease 19 (COVID-19) pandemic, keeping hands clean is especially important to help prevent the virus from spreading.

use sanitizer

use sanitizer which contain atleast 70% alcohol for better result, keep a small sanitizer along with you all the time or a spray type sanitizer.

wear a mask

Masks should be used as part of a comprehensive strategy of measures to suppress transmission and save lives; the use of a mask alone is not sufficient.

Take Vaccination

Take whatever vaccine is made available to you first, even if you have already had COVID-19. It is important to be vaccinated as soon as possible once it’s your turn and not wait.
